## RedStone

[RedStone](https://redstone.finance/) delivers frequently updated, reliable, and diverse data feeds for your dApp on Manta Pacific L2. RedStone is unique in many aspects, three notable ones are:
- The most gas-optimized oracle (make your dApp scalable)
- Unique price feeds (including LSTs, LRTs, RWAs and Manta-native assets)
- Modular Oracle Design allows for flexibility towards user needs

RedStone operates in two models Core (Pull) and Classic (Push) both available on Kava. Please see the specific steps and ready code samples in [the Docs](https://docs.redstone.finance/docs/smart-contract-devs/get-started/redstone-core).

All available Classic feeds are available in [here](https://docs.redstone.finance/docs/smart-contract-devs/price-feeds).

If you need help with integration join [RedStone Discord](https://discord.com/invite/PVxBZKFr46) and ask their team for help.
